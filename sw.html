Why make a new fan edit?

There are countless star wars edits. Unfortunately, most of them suffer from similar problems:

- Cut too much humour, leaving the movies overly serious
- Sgnificant alterations to the plot, creating a jarring tone for repeat viewers
- Too much cut screen time, creating a movie that's far too short
- Impressive from a creative level, they are unapproachable by a general audience.

Our edits takes queues from the best authors in the community who have mean’t to be viewed by just about everyone. Inspired by the professional work of HAL9000, Kitak, these edits draw from each to elevate each movie to it's fullest potential.
